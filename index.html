Course Overview

Introduction to Digital Marketing covers:

  • Search Engine Optimization (SEO)
  • Pay-Per-Click (PPC) Advertising
  • Social Media Marketing
  • Email Marketing
  • Analytics and Reporting

Each module includes video lectures, reading materials, and interactive quizzes to reinforce learning.

Course Modules

Module 1: Introduction to SEO

Learn the fundamentals of Search Engine Optimization and how to optimize websites for better visibility on search engines.

Module 2: PPC Advertising

Understand Pay-Per-Click advertising strategies on platforms like Google Ads and Bing Ads.

Module 3: Social Media Marketing

Explore effective strategies for engaging audiences on social media platforms.

Module 4: Email Marketing

Learn how to create and manage email marketing campaigns that drive engagement and conversions.

Module 5: Analytics and Reporting

Gain insights into analyzing digital marketing campaigns using tools like Google Analytics.
